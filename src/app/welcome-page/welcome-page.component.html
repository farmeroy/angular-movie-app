Welcome to Pre-Code Flix

Pre-Code Flix is a premium collection of movies from early Hollywood. The "Code" refers to the Hayes Code. Adopted in 1934 and in place until 1968, the Hayes Code enforced strict self-censorship in Hollywood, forbidding many things from the depictions of drug use and criminality to the satire of religious and political figures.
Pre-Code flix has all the best films from before this error of censorship.
